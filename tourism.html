About Us

Andhra Pradesh Tourism Corporation promotes the rich culture, history, and natural beauty of Andhra Pradesh. From the serene beaches of Visakhapatnam to the heritage sites of Amaravati, we ensure a memorable experience for every traveler.

Top Destinations

  • Visakhapatnam: Famous for its beaches and scenic beauty.
  • Tirupati: Renowned for the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple.
  • Araku Valley: A picturesque hill station with coffee plantations.
  • Amaravati: The ancient Buddhist heritage site.
